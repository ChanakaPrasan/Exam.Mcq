About Sahasra

Sahasra was built with a single mission: to democratize access to high-quality educational resources for every student in Sri Lanka. From rural schools to urban centers, we believe every child deserves the best preparation for their national examinations.

Our platform aggregates past papers, term test papers from leading national schools, and provincial examination papers, transforming them into interactive, engaging quizzes that provide instant feedback and comprehensive analytics.
